Course Overview

Chartered Accountant (CA) is India's most respected professional qualification in accounting and finance, regulated by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). The 4.5-year course combines articleship training with comprehensive study of accounting, auditing, taxation, corporate law, and financial management. CA graduates are highly sought after for financial advisory and assurance roles.

Core Subjects Covered

  • Financial Accounting & Reporting
  • Auditing & Assurance
  • Taxation (Income Tax & GST)
  • Corporate Law
  • Cost & Management Accounting
  • Financial Management
  • Advanced Accounting
  • Regulatory Framework
  • Business Strategy
  • Professional Ethics
