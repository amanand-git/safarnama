Course Overview

UPSC Civil Service Examination (CSE) preparation courses train candidates for India's most prestigious and challenging civil service exam. The course covers Indian polity, history, geography, economics, international relations, and current affairs. Successful candidates become IAS, IPS, IFS, and other civil service officers, serving in administrative and governance roles across India.

Exam Subjects & Topics

  • Indian Polity & Constitution
  • History of India
  • Geography of India
  • Economics & Development
  • Science & Technology
  • International Relations
  • Current Affairs & Politics
  • Ethics & Governance
  • Environmental Studies
  • General Knowledge & Analysis

Career Opportunities

  • IAS Officer (Indian Administrative Service)
  • IPS Officer (Indian Police Service)
  • IFS Officer (Indian Foreign Service)
  • IRS Officer (Indian Revenue Service)
  • District Magistrate
  • Police Commissioner
  • Foreign Service Officer
  • Defense Service Officer
  • Senior Government Position
  • Policy Making & Governance Role
