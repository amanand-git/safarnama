Course Overview

Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a prestigious postgraduate degree that equips students with advanced knowledge in business management, strategic planning, and leadership. The program focuses on developing critical thinking, decision-making, and problem-solving skills essential for senior management positions. MBA graduates are prepared to lead organizations across various industries and geographies.
