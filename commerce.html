Discipline Overview

Commerce is a dynamic discipline focused on business, finance, accounting, and economics. Commerce professionals work across various sectors including banking, accounting, corporate management, international trade, and finance. The field encompasses specializations such as accounting, finance, taxation, auditing, business management, and economics, providing diverse career opportunities in both public and private sectors globally.

Why Choose Commerce?

  • High earning potential in finance and business sectors
  • Multiple career paths in accounting, finance, and banking
  • Strong job demand and career stability
  • Professional certifications (CA, CS, CMA) valued globally
  • Entrepreneurial opportunities to start own business
  • International career prospects and mobility
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills development
  • Diverse industry opportunities across all sectors
