Course Overview

Journalism programs focus on reporting, fact-checking, media law, interviewing, editorial writing, and digital storytelling. Students learn to produce accurate and ethical news content across platforms. Practical newsroom exercises, internships, and portfolio publishing are central to career readiness.

Key Topics Covered

  • News Reporting Fundamentals
  • Media Ethics and Editorial Standards
  • Interviewing and Source Verification
  • Feature Writing and Opinion Formats
  • Digital Journalism and SEO Basics
  • Video Reporting and Mobile Journalism
  • Broadcast Script and Anchoring Basics
  • Data Journalism and Visual Storytelling
  • Media Law and Regulatory Awareness
  • Newsroom Workflow and Deadline Management
