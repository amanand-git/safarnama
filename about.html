MR. Aman Anand

Founder, Safarnama

Hello, I am Aman,

At 18 years old, most people are just starting to figure out their path. But for me, the path was always clear—I wanted to be an entrepreneur. I come from Kalyanpur, a small village in the Saran district of Bihar. It is a place where resources are often limited, but dreams are limitless.

Growing up in a rural environment, I witnessed a stark reality: talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. I saw brilliant students struggling not because they lacked intelligence, but because they lacked the right information. We didn't have easy access to career counselors, tech tools, or guidance on top colleges. I realized that in today's digital age, geography should not determine a student's destiny.

That realization became my driving force. I decided that instead of waiting for the system to change, I would build the solution myself.

This is how Safarnama was born.

Safarnama is not just a website; it is a digital ecosystem designed to make a student's life easier, smarter, and faster. My vision was to create a platform that serves as a one-stop destination for everything a learner needs:

Comprehensive Education Hub: From detailed information on colleges and courses to the latest updates in the education sector, I wanted to bring transparency to academic choices.

The Power of AI: I integrated Artificial Intelligence features to ensure that students get personalized, smart, and futuristic assistance in their studies.

Safareasy – The Utility Suite: I understand that students often struggle with technical tasks. That’s why I developed 'Safareasy', a suite of essential tools integrated right here. Whether you need an Image Compressor, a PDF Merger, or an Image Enhancer to fix your documents, you don’t need to visit ten different sites. It’s all here, free and accessible.

Building this from a village in Bihar wasn't easy. There were challenges, technical hurdles, and moments of doubt. But every time I coded a new feature or fixed a bug, I knew I was building something that could help millions of students like me.

I represent a new generation of Indian innovators who believe that you don't need a Silicon Valley address to make an impact. You just need a laptop, an internet connection, and an unbreakable will.

Thank you for being a part of my journey. I hope Safarnama helps you navigate your own path to success.

About Us

About Safarnama

Empowering Aspirants through Clarity and Data.

Welcome to Safarnama, a dedicated digital platform designed to simplify the complex world of Indian education and competitive examinations. In an era where information is abundant but often fragmented, Safarnama serves as a centralized hub for students, parents, and educators to find verified, real-time data on their academic journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To democratize access to educational information. Whether it is a prestigious government examination or a niche university course, we believe every student deserves a clear roadmap. We aim to eliminate confusion by providing structured insights into exam schedules, institutional rankings, and career opportunities.

What We Offer

Safarnama is built on the pillars of research and transparency. Our platform offers:

  • Government Examinations: UPSC, SSC, state-level exams with notifications, syllabus, and eligibility.
  • Institutional Rankings: Data-driven analysis of colleges and universities across India.
  • Course Discovery: Structured course insights aligned with career outcomes.

Why Safarnama?

  • Student-Centric Approach: Built by understanding real aspirant challenges.
  • Verified Data: Information sourced from official portals and gazettes.
  • Simple & Accessible: Clean interface with fast navigation.

Our Commitment

As India's educational ecosystem evolves, Safarnama evolves with it. We continuously expand our database, refine ranking methodologies, and improve accuracy to give aspirants a competitive edge.

Join Our Journey

Education is a marathon, not a sprint. Safarnama is here to walk that journey with you — offering clarity, data, and direction at every step.
