Course Overview

B.Sc programs provide strong foundations in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, and applied sciences. The course emphasizes practical laboratory exposure, conceptual depth, and project-based learning for industry and higher studies.

Key Topics Covered

  • Physics and Applied Concepts
  • Chemistry and Analytical Methods
  • Mathematics and Statistics
  • Biology and Life Science Basics
  • Laboratory Safety and Practical Work
  • Scientific Computing Fundamentals
  • Data Interpretation
  • Mini Projects and Viva
