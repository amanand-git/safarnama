Course Overview

Bachelor of Science in Agriculture is a 4-year undergraduate program combining theoretical knowledge with practical field experience. Students learn crop production, soil science, plant pathology, irrigation, and sustainable farming practices. The program prepares graduates for careers in agriculture, food production, research, and agribusiness.

Core Subjects Covered

  • Crop Production & Management
  • Soil Science & Fertility
  • Plant Pathology
  • Agricultural Chemistry
  • Irrigation & Water Management
  • Agricultural Economics
  • Organic Farming
