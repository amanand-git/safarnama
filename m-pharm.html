Course Overview

M.Pharm focuses on advanced pharmaceutical sciences after B.Pharm. Students choose specializations such as Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Quality Assurance, and Regulatory Affairs. The course emphasizes lab research, analytical methods, thesis work, and industry-ready quality systems.

Key Topics Covered

  • Advanced Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmacology and Toxicology
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Medicinal and Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Drug Regulatory Affairs
  • Quality Assurance and GMP
  • Biopharmaceutics and Pharmacokinetics
  • Clinical Pharmacy Orientation
  • Research Methodology and Biostatistics
  • Dissertation and Lab Thesis
