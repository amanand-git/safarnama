Course Overview

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a prestigious 4-year undergraduate degree that equips students with comprehensive knowledge in engineering and technology. The program combines theoretical learning with practical hands-on experience through laboratories, projects, and internships. B.Tech graduates are trained to solve complex engineering problems, innovate, and lead technological advancement in various industries including aerospace, automotive, information technology, civil infrastructure, and beyond.
