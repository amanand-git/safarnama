Discipline Overview

Pharmacy is a comprehensive discipline that combines chemistry, biology, and healthcare to develop, produce, and distribute medicines that improve human health. Pharmacists are essential healthcare professionals who work in various settings including hospitals, retail pharmacies, pharmaceutical industries, research centers, and regulatory agencies. The field offers diverse career paths ranging from clinical pharmacy and drug development to pharmaceutical research and regulatory affairs.
