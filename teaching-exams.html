Course Overview

Teaching exam preparation requires pedagogy understanding, subject command, and exam-specific strategy. This track aligns with official patterns used in major exams like CTET and UGC NET. Students prepare through syllabus mapping, chapter-wise tests, NCERT revision, pedagogy frameworks, and previous year paper analysis. Focus remains on qualifying eligibility tests first, then building profile strength for recruitment.

Official Exam Snapshot (Verified)

  • CTET has two papers: Paper I for Classes I-V and Paper II for Classes VI-VIII.
  • Each CTET paper has 150 multiple-choice questions for 150 marks.
  • CTET duration is 150 minutes (2.5 hours) per paper.
  • CTET has no negative marking.
  • CTET qualifying benchmark is 60% and above (as cited in official bulletin based on NCTE notification).
  • CTET qualifying certificate validity period is lifetime.
  • UGC NET pattern: Paper I (50 questions, 100 marks) and Paper II (100 questions, 200 marks).
  • UGC NET total exam duration is 180 minutes with no break and no negative marking.
  • UGC NET qualification categories include JRF + Assistant Professor, Assistant Professor + PhD, and PhD admission only.
  • NCTE TET guideline framework was updated through amendment notifications including the 09 June 2021 amendment.
