Course Overview

Bachelor of Nursing (B.Sc Nursing) is a 4-year undergraduate program combining theoretical knowledge with practical clinical training. Students learn patient care, medical-surgical nursing, community health, mental health nursing, and pediatric nursing. The program prepares graduates to work as registered nurses in hospitals, clinics, community health centers, and other healthcare settings.

Core Subjects Covered

  • Fundamentals of Nursing
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing
  • Community Health Nursing
  • Pediatric Nursing
  • Mental Health Nursing
  • Obstetric & Gynecological Nursing
  • Pharmacology
  • Microbiology
  • Anatomy & Physiology
  • Nursing Research & Management
