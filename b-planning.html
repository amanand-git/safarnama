Course Overview

B.Planning trains students in city planning, urban policy, GIS mapping, mobility planning, and infrastructure analysis. The program combines technical tools with social, economic, and environmental perspectives.

Key Topics Covered

  • Urban Planning Fundamentals
  • Land Use and Zoning
  • GIS and Spatial Mapping
  • Transport Planning Basics
  • Housing and Infrastructure
  • Urban Governance and Policy
  • Sustainability in Cities
  • Planning Studio and Surveys
