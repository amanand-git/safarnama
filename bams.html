Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS)

Learn classical Ayurveda with modern clinical training. BAMS builds a strong foundation in holistic healing, preventive care, and patient-centered practice.

Ayurveda and Integrative Care

Course Overview

BAMS is the main professional degree for Ayurvedic medicine in India. The curriculum blends ancient Ayurvedic principles with contemporary anatomy, pathology, and clinical exposure. Students learn diagnosis through dosha-based understanding, herbal and mineral formulations, panchakarma therapies, and community healthcare. The final internship gives practical exposure in hospitals and Ayurvedic clinics.

Key Topics Covered

  • Padartha Vigyan and Ayurvedic Philosophy
  • Samhita and Siddhanta Foundations
  • Rachana Sharir and Kriya Sharir
  • Dravyaguna Vigyan and Rasashastra
  • Roga Nidana and Vikriti Vigyan
  • Kayachikitsa and Panchakarma
  • Shalya and Shalakya Tantra Basics
  • Kaumarbhritya and Prasuti Tantra
  • Swasthavritta and Community Health
  • Clinical Posting and Case Management
