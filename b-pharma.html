Course Overview

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharmacy) is a 4-year professional degree combining chemistry, biology, and healthcare sciences. Students learn drug development, pharmaceutical analysis, pharmacology, and pharmacy practice. The program prepares graduates for careers in pharmaceutical industries, hospitals, clinical practice, and research.
