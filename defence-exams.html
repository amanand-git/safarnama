Course Overview

Defence exam preparation combines academic performance and officer-like personality. This track is built around official exam structures of UPSC NDA/CDS and defence services recruitment systems. You prepare for written papers, SSB process, documentation, and fitness standards with a realistic timetable. The objective is not just clearing written exams, but securing final merit through consistent preparation across all stages.

Official Exam Snapshot (Verified)

  • UPSC Annual Calendar 2026 lists NDA and CDS (I) exam date as 12 April 2026, and NDA and CDS (II) as 13 September 2026.
  • NDA written exam: Mathematics (300 marks) + General Ability Test (600 marks), each paper 2.5 hours, objective type.
  • NDA Services Selection Board (SSB) test carries 900 marks.
  • CDS written exam (IMA, INA, AFA): English, General Knowledge, Elementary Mathematics (100 marks each, 2 hours each).
  • CDS written exam (OTA): English and General Knowledge (100 marks each, 2 hours each).
  • CDS SSB interview marks: 300 for IMA/INA/AFA and 200 for OTA.
  • Agniveer (Navy) entry age band is 17.5 to 21 years, as per official Navy recruitment details.
  • Agniveer engagement period is 4 years, with up to 25% selection for regular cadre based on organizational requirement and policy.
