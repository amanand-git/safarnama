Course Overview

Entrepreneurship Development course teaches aspiring entrepreneurs the essential skills to launch and manage successful businesses. The program covers business planning, market research, financial management, and growth strategies. Students work on live projects and develop business plans that can be implemented, making them ready to become job creators rather than job seekers.
