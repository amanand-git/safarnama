Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Build AI systems that understand, generate, and reason over human language. Learn modern NLP pipelines from preprocessing to LLM-powered applications.

4-7 Months
Intermediate to Advanced
AI & Language Tech
Online/Offline

Course Overview

NLP is one of the fastest-moving areas of applied AI, powering chatbots, search, translation, summarization, and voice assistants. This course covers both classical NLP methods and transformer-based deep learning. You will build projects with embeddings, retrieval pipelines, and LLM workflows while learning responsible AI practices for quality, safety, and bias control.

Key Topics Covered

  • Text Preprocessing, Tokenization, and Linguistic Normalization
  • Word Embeddings, Sentence Embeddings, and Vector Similarity
  • Transformer Architecture: Attention, BERT, and GPT Foundations
  • Prompt Engineering and Instruction Tuning Basics
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Vector Databases
  • Text Classification, Sentiment Analysis, and Intent Detection
  • Named Entity Recognition and Information Extraction
  • Machine Translation, Summarization, and Question Answering
  • NLP Evaluation Metrics: BLEU, ROUGE, F1, and Human Evaluation
  • Responsible AI for NLP: Hallucination, Safety, and Bias Mitigation
