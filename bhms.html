Course Overview

BHMS is the core professional degree for homeopathic practice in India. The course trains students in medical fundamentals, homeopathic materia medica, repertory use, and clinical diagnostics. Students learn to evaluate symptoms comprehensively and design individualized care plans. Internship training builds real-world patient interaction and treatment experience.

Key Topics Covered

  • Human Anatomy, Physiology, and Biochemistry
  • Pathology and Microbiology Foundations
  • Organon of Medicine and Homeopathic Philosophy
  • Homeopathic Materia Medica
  • Repertory and Case Analysis
  • Forensic Medicine and Toxicology Basics
  • Community Medicine and Public Health
  • Medicine, Surgery, and Obstetrics Orientation
  • Clinical Homeopathy and OPD/IPD Posting
  • Internship-based Patient Management
