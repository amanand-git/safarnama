Course Overview

SSC CGL is one of the most popular recruitment exams for Group B and Group C posts in ministries and government departments. This preparation track focuses on concept clarity, question selection strategy, time management, and revision cycles. Students practice exam-level sets for Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, English, General Awareness, and Computer Knowledge with regular full-length mocks.

Key Topics Covered

  • Quantitative Aptitude: Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, and DI
  • General Intelligence and Reasoning: Verbal and Non-Verbal
  • English Comprehension: Grammar, Vocabulary, and Reading Skills
  • General Awareness: History, Polity, Economy, Geography, and Current Affairs
  • Computer Knowledge: Basics, Internet, Security, and MS Office
  • Previous Year Papers and Chapter-wise Practice Sets
  • Tier-I and Tier-II Mock Tests with Performance Analysis
  • Shortcuts, Calculation Speed, and Accuracy Improvement
  • Data Entry Test Guidance and Practice Framework
  • Revision Planning and Last-Month Strategy
