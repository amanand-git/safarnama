Course Overview

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is a prestigious 3-year undergraduate degree that provides comprehensive knowledge in commerce, accounting, finance, economics, and business management. The program combines theoretical foundations with practical skills training through case studies, projects, and internships. B.Com graduates are equipped with expertise in financial management, taxation, auditing, and business analysis, making them valuable professionals in corporate sector, finance institutions, and government organizations.
