Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)

Prepare for professional teaching roles with pedagogy, classroom management, and curriculum implementation skills.

2 Years
Professional Teaching Degree
Pedagogy and School Education

Course Overview

B.Ed prepares learners for structured school teaching careers through educational psychology, pedagogy, curriculum design, and school internship practice. It builds both instructional competence and classroom readiness.

Key Topics Covered

  • Educational Psychology
  • Teaching-Learning Methods
  • Curriculum and Evaluation
  • Classroom Management
  • Inclusive Education
  • ICT in Teaching
  • Assessment Design
  • School Internship
