Course Overview

Mass Communication programs train students for journalism, advertising, public relations, broadcasting, and digital content industries. The curriculum blends media theory with practical production, writing, camera work, editing, campaign design, and communication strategy. Internships and portfolio building are key components.

Key Topics Covered

  • Media and Communication Theory
  • Public Relations and Corporate Communication
  • Advertising Strategy and Campaigns
  • Broadcast Production Basics
  • Digital Content and Social Media Strategy
  • Script Writing and Storyboarding
  • Audio-Video Editing Fundamentals
  • Media Research and Audience Analytics
  • Media Law and Ethics
  • Brand Communication and Campaign Execution
