Course Overview

Paramedical education prepares students for vital roles that support doctors and hospital systems. Programs include medical lab technology, radiology, operation theatre technology, emergency care, dialysis, optometry, and other specialized tracks. The curriculum focuses on practical skills, clinical protocols, infection control, and patient management, making it one of the fastest entry routes into healthcare employment.

Key Topics Covered

  • Human Anatomy and Physiology Fundamentals
  • Clinical Biochemistry and Diagnostic Procedures
  • Medical Laboratory Techniques and Reporting
  • Radiology and Imaging Support Basics
  • Operation Theatre and Sterilization Protocols
  • Emergency and Trauma Care Basics
  • Dialysis and Critical Care Support
  • Patient Monitoring and Vital Management
  • Infection Control and Biomedical Waste Handling
  • Medical Ethics, Documentation, and Communication
