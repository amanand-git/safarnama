Animation and Visual Effects

Create engaging visual stories through 2D and 3D animation, motion graphics, and VFX production workflows.

1-4 Years (course-wise)

2D, 3D Animation and VFX

Course Overview

Animation programs teach storytelling, character design, rigging, motion principles, visual effects, and post-production workflows. Students build portfolios with short films and showreels for media and gaming careers.

Key Topics Covered

  • Principles of Animation
  • Storyboarding and Narrative
  • Character Design and Modeling
  • 2D and 3D Animation Tools
  • Rigging and Motion Workflows
  • Lighting and Rendering Basics
  • Compositing and VFX
  • Portfolio and Showreel Development
