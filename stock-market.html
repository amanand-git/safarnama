Stock Market and Trading

Understand equity markets, trading systems, risk management, and long-term investing with practical market analysis skills.

Course Overview

Stock market training covers core market structure, technical and fundamental analysis, portfolio strategy, trading psychology, and risk control. Students learn how exchanges work, how to read financial data, and how to design disciplined trading or investing plans. The emphasis should remain on risk-managed decision making rather than speculation.

Key Topics Covered

  • Stock Market Basics and Participants
  • Exchange Structure and Order Types
  • Technical Analysis Fundamentals
  • Fundamental Analysis and Financial Statements
  • Risk Management and Position Sizing
  • Swing, Intraday, and Positional Strategies
  • Derivatives Introduction
  • Portfolio Building and Asset Allocation
  • Trading Psychology and Discipline
  • Tax and Compliance Awareness
